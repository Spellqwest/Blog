# Blog 1: First beginnings 🎉

**Hi everyone!** 👋  
This is the **first** of many upcoming blog posts. These posts are a **way to share our thoughts, ideas, and experiences** with the other teams. They will also contain **updates on the progress** of the project, as well as the **distribution of work** between the team members.
